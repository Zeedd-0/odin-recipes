Feijoada Recipe

Description

A very tradional brazilian dish made with black beans and pork pieces. In its traditional form, the pork pieces used are tongue, ears, feet, tail and belly.

Usually made by restaurants at Wednesdays and Saturdays.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of Black beans
  • 70g of Pork ribs
  • 70g of Pork feet
  • 70g of Pork ears
  • 70g of Pork tail
  • 100g of Wiener
  • 2 Onions

Steps

  1. Put the meat on the water for 36 hours, changing the water time for time.
  2. Start cooking the hard meat first, then add the soft ones.
  3. Once all meat are well cooked and soft, take them out and start cooking the beans in the same water.
  4. Season the the beans and once they are cooked too, you can add the meat together.
